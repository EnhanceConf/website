About Sponsor Speakers CFP 

We're convinced progressive enhancement remains an important aspect in pushing the boundaries of the web while still providing a robust experience for every user.

EnhanceConf will cover the state of the art in progressive enhancement; focussing on providing the best experience to the widest possible audience.

Topics we want to cover include:

  • Application architectures
  • Isomorphic apps
  • Offline apps
  • Device/network-agnostic design
  • Progressive apps
  • Baselines
  • Mustard tests
  • ROCA
  • Tooling

A call for proposals will open in the next few months. EnhanceConf may dive into a particular language, library or framework but the focus will be on techniques rather than specific implementations.

EnhanceConf has a Code of Conduct. It will be enforced. Any extra money from ticket sales will be put back into the event.
