About Sponsor Speakers 

We're convinced progressive enhancement remains an important aspect in pushing the boundaries of the web while still providing a robust experience for every user.

We wanted a conference covering the state of the art in progressive enhancement; focussing on providing the best experience to the widest possible audience.

Topics we want to cover include:

  • Application architectures
  • Isomorphic apps
  • Offline apps
  • Device/network-agnostic design
  • ROCA
  • Progressive apps

EnhanceConf will be a community event, any extra money from ticket sales will be put back into the event.

We may dive into a particular language, library or framework but the focus will be on techniques rather than specific implementations.

EnhanceConf has a Code of Conduct. It will be enforced.
