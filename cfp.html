Call for Proposals

EnhanceConf is a one-day, single-track conference.

We would really like to hear stories about how progressive enhancement effects end users. If you think you might have something worth sharing please get in touch!

Some of the topics we would particularly like to hear about include:

  • Application architectures
  • Offline apps
  • Device/network-agnostic design
  • Progressive apps
  • Baselines
  • Mustard tests
  • Tooling
  • Relevance in the modern web
  • UX implications
  • Accessibility
  • ROCA
  • Web Components
  • Service Workers
  • Isomorphic JavaScript
  • Sharing templates between server and client

Did we miss something off the list? Please let us know.

Guidelines:

  • Talks will be 20 minutes long with time for questions.
  • Speakers are asked to read and comply with our code of conduct.

Please email your submissions to info@enhanceconf.com with 'EnhanceConf Talk Proposal' as the subject.

If you have any questions feel free to email us or reach out on twitter @enhanceconf.

The Process:

There are 9 speaking slots available.

All proposal must be received by 20 December 2015. We will notify all submissions by January 20th 2016 if their talks have been selected on not.

We are happy to help develop your ideas into a proposal. Even if its your first proposal please send it our way.
